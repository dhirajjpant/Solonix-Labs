Careers at SolonixLabs

We are building a small, high-caliber engineering team focused on shipping real AI systems into production environments.

Who this is for

Roles at SolonixLabs are for people who want to work on real systems, real data, and real constraints — not toy problems or endless experiments.

If you enjoy ownership, ambiguity, and writing code that will be used in production, you will fit in well here.

Open positions

Full-Stack Engineering Intern

Work across frontend and backend on AI-powered web applications used by real organizations.

What you will work on
• User interfaces for AI systems
• Backend APIs and integrations
• Authentication, data flow, and system reliability

Strong requirements
• Solid understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript Frameworks like React & Next.js
• Experience building complete web applications
• Comfort with at least one backend stack (Node.js or Python)
• Understanding of REST APIs and databases

Bonus
• Deployment experience (Docker, cloud, CI/CD)

AI / Machine Learning Intern

Contribute to applied ML systems used in production — from data pipelines to model evaluation and deployment support.

What you will work on
• Data preparation and evaluation pipelines
• Model experimentation and analysis
• Supporting deployment and monitoring

Strong requirements
• Strong Python skills
• Hands-on experience with ML libraries
• Understanding of core ML concepts
• Experience working with real, messy datasets

Bonus
• NLP, computer vision, or LLM-based systems
• Familiarity with MLOps concepts

What we expect

We expect professionalism, curiosity, and ownership. You should be comfortable asking questions, proposing solutions, and taking responsibility for your work.

Strong performers may be considered for extended roles or long-term collaboration.
